ECT devices are categorized as Class II devices for treating catatonia or severe major depressive episodes linked with major depressive disorder or bipolar disorder in patients aged 13 years and older who exhibit treatment resistance or necessitate a rapid response due to the severity of their psychiatric or medical condition.
